Sine Easing

Sine easing in GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform) creates smooth, natural motion by mimicking the curve of a sine wave. It starts and/or ends the animation gradually, perfect for subtle, organic transitions.

Common variants include sine.in, sine.out, and sine.inOut, each controlling the pace of acceleration and deceleration.
